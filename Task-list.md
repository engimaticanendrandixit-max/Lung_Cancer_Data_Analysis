# 📋 Task List

This document contains the list of analysis tasks performed on the lung cancer dataset.

---

# Task 1: Age at Diagnosis vs Cancer Stage

## Research Question

How does the age at diagnosis vary across different cancer stages (Stage I–IV)?

## Work to be Performed

* Calculate the mean age for each cancer stage.
* Calculate the median age for each cancer stage.
* Compare age distributions across stages.
* Visualize the results using:

  * Boxplot
  * Violin plot

---

# Task 2: Country-wise Cancer Stage Distribution

## Research Question

Do different countries show different distributions of cancer stages at diagnosis?

## Work to be Performed

* Calculate the percentage distribution of cancer stages for each country.
* Compare stage prevalence across countries.
* Identify country-wise patterns.
* Visualize the results using:

  * Bar chart
  * Normalized bar chart

---

# Task 3: Treatment Type Selection by Cancer Stage

## Research Question

How do treatment choices differ across cancer stages?

## Work to be Performed

* Calculate treatment frequencies for each cancer stage.
* Compare treatment strategies.
* Study how treatment selection changes with stage.
* Visualize the results using:

  * Count plot
  * Grouped bar chart

---

# Task 4: Smoking Status vs Age at Diagnosis

## Research Question

Are smokers diagnosed with lung cancer at a younger age compared to non-smokers?

## Work to be Performed

* Separate patients according to smoking status.
* Calculate:

  * Mean age
  * Median age
  * Standard deviation
* Compare age distributions.
* Visualize the results using:

  * Boxplot

---

# Task 5: Multimorbidity Prevalence Analysis

## Research Question

How common is multimorbidity among lung cancer patients?

## Work to be Performed

* Combine the following conditions:

  * Hypertension
  * Asthma
  * Cirrhosis
  * Other Cancer
* Calculate the number of additional diseases.
* Analyze multimorbidity prevalence.
* Visualize the results using:

  * Count plot
  * Summary statistics

---

# Expected Outcome

This analysis aims to provide insights into:

* Age variation across cancer stages.
* Country-wise stage distributions.
* Treatment selection patterns.
* Smoking effects on diagnosis age.
* Prevalence of multimorbidity.
